OneSixty Fundraising: Fundraiser and Team Leader

London — Jan 2019 to May 2019

I worked full-time as a face-to-face fundraiser on the streets of London. It was my job to encourage passers-by to donate to charities such as UNICEF and Centrepoint on a recurring, monthly basis. I also had the chance to raise funds on behalf of Teenage Cancer Trust during their annual gig at the Royal Albert Hall. Working on behalf of these causes allowed me to collaborate with others and develop sales techniques. My managers awarded me for employee of the month twice before I became a Team Leader. I left following difficult encounters with members of the public.
